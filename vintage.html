Our Story

London's Premier
Motor House

Metropolitan Motors was established in 1949 by Mr. Harold Ashworth on Brompton Road, Kensington, with a singular vision: to bring the finest European motorcars to the discerning British motorist.

We are the exclusive London representatives for Fiat of Turin, Peugeot of Paris, and Austin of Birmingham — three marques at the absolute forefront of modern automobile engineering.

Every vehicle in our showroom has been personally inspected and road-tested. We offer full manufacturer warranty, instalment purchase plans through Lombard Banking, and a comprehensive after-sales service.
